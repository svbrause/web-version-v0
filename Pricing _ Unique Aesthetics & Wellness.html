Tetra CO2 Laser

The Tetra CO2 laser is a state-of-the-art fractional CO2 laser device. This advanced technology offers a wide range of benefits for skin rejuvenation, resurfacing, and tightening, making it a powerful tool for achieving exceptional skin results. CO2 lasers are the gold standard in treating wrinkles, age spots, acne scars, and other blemishes as well as tightening skin and balancing tone. Damaged skin tissue is removed, stimulating collagen production and heat energy is delivered to tighten the skin. The Tetra Pro CO2 laser system stands out for its versatility, allowing us to provide both deep skin resurfacing (DEKA Pulse Treatment) and light superficial resurfacing (Cool Peel).

The Scarlet SRF uses short-pulse radio frequency (SRF) with microneedling to increase the skin’s firmness and decrease the appearance of aging skin.
